Master English Grammar

Understand sentence structures and grammar rules with ease.

Simple Present Tense

Used for habits, general facts, or schedules.

Example: "I eat breakfast every morning."

"The sun rises in the east."

Verb 'to be' (am/is/are)

To express identity, condition, or location.

Example: "I am happy."

"She is a doctor."

"They are in the park."

Basic Sentence Structure

Subject + Verb + Object (S-V-O).

Example: "He (S) plays (V) soccer (O)."

"She (S) reads (V) a book (O)."

Personal Pronouns

*Possessive adjectives are used before nouns (e.g., "my book"). Possessive pronouns stand alone (e.g., "The book is mine").
